About Us

Team Nequit is an official formula student team of Rajarambapu Institute of Technology. We design, manufacture and test formula style race car within campus against teams from all over India. Every year we participate in two international events that are Formula Bharat and SAE Supra. We work as a family to achieve our goals. Our team was established in the year 2014 by our founding member Akshay Chavan. Team has come a long way from its beginnings . We aim to dream big and follow the concept of one team, one dream, one car which induce us to manufacture combustion as well as an electric vehicle.


Mission & Vision

Mission:-

To grab the podium finish at Formula Bharat and SAE Supra.

To give chance to apply classroom learning in the outside world.

To technically nouish the students by combin ing their skills.


Vision:-

To create a legacy of a winning team at every Indian and International event.

To create industrial environment and building up engineers following industrial practices.
